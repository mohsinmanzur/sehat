From paper to pocket in four steps

Sehat bridges the gap between your physical documents and your doctor's screen with simple steps.

Scan Documents

Take a picture of a lab report. Sehat structures metrics such as Hemoglobin or Fasting Glucose values, avoiding tedious manual entry.

Local Records

Verify extracted metrics on your dashboard, building a continuous chronological database of your metrics — even offline.

Doctor Requests

During clinic visits, your doctor requests access to specific charts via the Sehat Doctor portal.

Consent Grant

Approve the request with a single slide to generate a temporary secure passcode, shared instantly with your doctor.

Designed around how you actually use it

Three defaults that make tracking sustainable and keep your data exactly where you want it.

Insightful Trends

Stop looking at isolated numbers. Track Blood Pressure, Sugar, and Heart Rate on dashboard charts that show what's changed since your last reading.

Offline-First by Design

Log a reading, view your history, or open a scanned report with zero signal. Everything you add offline syncs automatically once you're back online.

Device-Only Mode

Prefer nothing to leave your phone at all? One switch in Settings pauses all cloud sync and keeps every record strictly local.

Security First

Privacy-first, by architecture

We design Sehat around patient consent and structured access tokens, so your records remain entirely in your control.

Locked by Default

Every record is private the moment it's scanned — invisible to anyone else until you actively grant access.

Time-Bound Access

Every share you approve carries an expiry. Access ends automatically, or the moment you tap Revoke.

Your Device, Your Data

Switch on Device-Only Mode and Sehat stops syncing entirely — your records never leave your phone.
