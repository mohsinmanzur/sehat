Core Capabilities

Features designed for patient autonomy

Sehat provides standard tracking controls and privacy guarantees to empower your health journey.

AI-Powered Digitization

Upload a photo or PDF of any paper lab report. Our localized AI model scans, extracts variables, and structures values into visual tracking records automatically.

Consent Access Control

Your records remain locked by default. Doctors request access through custom parameters, which you approve via time-bound, self-expiring secure passcodes.

Insightful Trends

Stop looking at isolated numbers. Monitor chronological metrics (Blood Sugar, Pressure, Heart Rate) and review health summaries through elegant dashboard charts.

Workflow

How Sehat Operates

Sehat bridges the gap between your physical documents and your doctor’s screen with simple steps.

Step 1

Scan Documents

Take a picture of a lab document. Sehat structures metrics such as Hemoglobin or Fasting Glucose values, avoiding tedious manual entry.

Step 2

Local Records

Verify extracted metrics on your dashboard, building a continuous chronological database of your metrics.

Step 3

Doctor Requests

During clinic visits, your doctor requests access to specific charts via the Sehat Doctor portal.

Step 4

Consent Grant

Approve the request to generate a temporary secure passcode. Your data is encrypted and immediately made available to the doctor.

Security First

Privacy-First Consent Architecture

We design Sehat with local database storage, patient consent, and structured access tokens. We ensure your records remain entirely in your control.

Consent-based validation checks
Time-bound, self-revoking permissions
Zero permanent doctor storage
Comprehensive immutable audit logging
Guaranteed Control No health metrics are shared without your active slide approval. Revoke keys at any time.
